Educational History

My name is David Lopez and I was born and raised in Brooklyn, NY. Most of my school life was done in public school. Public Schools in Brooklyn can be an adventure.

For middle school, or Junior High School in my day, I went to Junior High School 50. The school did not have the greatest reputation but my class managed to make it one of the greatest school experiences.

Murry Bergtraum High School for Business Careers was home for my highschool years. I breezed through high school and had only 3 classes my last year. With so much time, I became a social bug, going to parties, meeting new people, and learning how to network for business purposes.
